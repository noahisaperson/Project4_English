The Cost of Digitalization

Young kids use digital technology much more than recommended
American Academy of Pediatrics recommended screen time per day for children 8 - 12:
2 hours

Actual average screen time per day for children 8 - 12:
4 hours, 36 minutes
This overuse of digital technology has been shown to directly or indirectly cause:
  • Diabetes
  • Developmental Delays
  • Sleep Disorders
  • ADHD
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
